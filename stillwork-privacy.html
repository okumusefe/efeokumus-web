Privacy Policy

Stillwork: Focus Timer  ·  Last updated: April 2025

Overview

Stillwork: Focus Timer ("the App") is developed and maintained by Efe Okumuş ("I", "me", or "my"). This Privacy Policy explains what information the App collects, how it is used, and your rights as a user. I am committed to protecting your privacy.

Information I Do Not Collect

Stillwork does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. Specifically:

  • No account or registration is required.
  • No personally identifiable information is collected.
  • No usage data or analytics are sent to any server.
  • No location data is accessed or collected.
  • No third-party analytics or advertising SDKs are included.

Data Stored on Your Device

All app data — such as your timer settings and session preferences — is stored locally on your device using Apple's standard storage APIs. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to me or any third party.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party services, trackers, or advertising networks. It does not make any network requests.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Because no data is collected at all, the App is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If I make any changes to this Privacy Policy, the updated version will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date. I encourage you to review this page periodically.
