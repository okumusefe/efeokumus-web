Frequently Asked Questions

How do I unlock Premium features?

Tap the "Upgrade" button in the Settings tab. Stillwork Premium is a one-time purchase — no subscription.

I purchased Premium but my features are locked. What do I do?

Go to Settings and tap "Restore Purchases" to restore your purchase.

Does Stillwork sync across my devices?

Stillwork stores all data locally on your device. There is no cloud sync.

How do I change the timer duration?

Timer duration can be customized after upgrading to Stillwork Premium via the Settings tab.

How do I cancel or get a refund?

Since Stillwork Premium is a one-time purchase managed by Apple, refund requests should be directed to Apple at reportaproblem.apple.com.
